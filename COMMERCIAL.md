## FAQ

**What counts as commercial use?**

- Running AutoAW as a paid or free SaaS product for your customers
- Embedding AutoAW in a product or service you sell or monetize
- Using AutoAW internally at a for-profit company to automate work that generates revenue
- Offering AutoAW as a managed service or API

**What is free?**

- Personal side projects with no monetization
- Academic research (at a university or research institution)
- Evaluation and testing before you decide whether to buy a license
- Non-profit organizations
- Open source projects (that are themselves noncommercial)

**I'm a researcher at a for-profit company. Do I need a license?**

If the research is purely internal, non-commercial, and not directly tied to a revenue-generating product, it's free. If in doubt, email us — we're happy to clarify or grant a free research license.

**Can I fork and modify AutoAW?**

Yes, under the same noncommercial terms. Forks must include the original license and a notice of your modifications.

**What does a commercial license include?**

- Permission to use AutoAW in commercial products and services
- No copyleft obligations
- Email support SLA (terms depend on tier)

Pricing is based on company size and use case. Email **spirtik87@gmail.com** to discuss.
